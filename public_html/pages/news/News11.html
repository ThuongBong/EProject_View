Photographs: Hanoi people pulled together to the sunflower garden in Thang Long Royal Palace photographed
Posted on October 11,2017 by admin
-No need to go far away, this year's capital city people can see the sunflower gardens to 2000m2 blooming Thang Long Imperial Citadel.

The duration of the flower season usually only lasts 15-20 days, but the sunflowers in Thang Long Imperial City will be fertilized to the Lunar New Year to serve the spring demand of people.


It is known that the sunflower garden was planted 2 months ago, after much effort to fertilize brilliantly spread in the enjoyment of many people.


The garden attracts many people, both adults and children to visit, take photos.


Everyone was eager to be photographed in the middle of the flower garden.
