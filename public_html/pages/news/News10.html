Citadel of Ho Dynasty, unique works in Vietnam
Posted on October 11,2017 by admin
-Built on an average of 1.5 m long, with a plate up to 6 m, stacked without adhesive, over 600 years, the walls surrounding Citadel of Ho are still intact. .

Citadel of Ho Dynasty is located in Vinh Tien and Vinh Long communes (Vinh Loc, Thanh Hoa). This is unique stone architecture is unique in Vietnam. Built by Ho Quy Ly in 1397, this town is also known as Tay Do (or Tay Giai) to distinguish it from Dong Do (Thang Long - Hanoi). After completion, Ho Quy Ly moved the capital from Thang Long to Tay Do.

According to historical records, in 1397, at the risk of Minh's invasion from the North, Ho Quy Ly chose An Ton (now Vinh Loc, Thanh Hoa) to build a city in preparation for a war. Long-term resistance, at the same time, is also a way to cheer the people with the Tran.

The gate to the south is the main entrance to the Imperial Citadel. Picture: Le Hoang.

Selected land is located in the area between Ma River and Buoi River, in the north there is Tu Tuong Mountain, in the west there is Nguu Ngu Mountain, in the east there is Mount Khuyen, in the south is a convergence of Ma River and Buoi River.

The Ho Dynasty consists of 3 parts, La Thanh, Hao Thanh and Hoang Thanh. La Thanh is the outer ring, perimeter about 4 km. The wall is dug around the four outer city, sincere way in the direction of about 50 m. This work is to protect the city.

The town was built on a square with a square shape. North - South length of 870.5 m, East - West length of 883.5 m. The four gates in the south - north - west - east direction are called Tien - Hau - Ta - Huu gates. Each door is open in the middle. These ports are built in dome architecture. The slabs of stone on the archways of the grapefruit, clustered together on top of each other.

Money gate (south gate) is the main gate, there are three gates. The middle door is 5.82 meters wide, 5.75 meters high, two side doors 5.45 meters wide, 5.35 meters high. The remaining three gates have only one door. The wall height is 5-6 m high, the highest is the 10 m high gate.

The Nam Nhai Gate (Royal Road) is connected with the South Gate, which is about 2.5 km long to the Nam Giao River (where the King's sacrifice was built) in August 1402.

Over 600 years of history and weather, the outer wall is intact. Picture: Le Hoang.

The entire wall and four main gates were built of blue limestone slabs, carefully chiseled, squared, stacked together. The stones are 1.5 m long and have a length of up to 6 m, weighing 24 tons. The total volume of rock used is about 20,000 m3 and nearly 100,000 m3 of soil is elaborately excavated.

Heavy stones weighing only a ton are stacked up without adhesives to ensure durability. Over the past 600 years with the ups and downs of history and the effects of weather, the wall system is quite intact, although the construction time is very fast, only in about 3 months.

According to the history of the city, many buildings were built, such as Hoang Nguyen palace, Dien Tho palace (the residence of Ho Quy Ly), Eastern palace, western Thai temple, east Thai temple, Tho Ky mountain, Duc Tuong. very dear, like the capital of Thang Long.

However, over 600 years of existence, most of the architecture inside the Imperial Citadel was destroyed. The vestiges of the old palace are still hidden beneath the paddy fields of the people around the area.

Among the thousands of artifacts unearthed in the Ho Dynasty, there are many types of weapons including stone bullets, four-sided iron, knives, arrows, nails ... Photo: Le Hoang.

On June 27th, at the 35th session of the World Heritage Committee of the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) in Paris (France), the Ho recognized as world cultural heritage.

Mr. Vuong Van Viet, Vice Chairman of Thanh Hoa People's Committee said that in the coming time, the province will implement synchronous solutions to protect and promote the historical value of this monument. In the immediate future, the province will implement the preservation, renovation and embellishment in accordance with the Law on Cultural Heritage of Vietnam and the International Convention on World Heritages, continue to formulate and submit to the Prime Minister for approval the conservation plan. , restoration of this heritage.

The province will coordinate with the authorities to step by step excavate archeology, call for investment resources to increase the attraction of attractions and attractions. "Together with Hue ancient capital, Thang Long citadel, Thanh Hoa province is very proud and proud to have a city recognized as a cultural heritage of the world," he emphasized.
