Details Itinerary

Day 1:Ho Chi Minh - Edinburgh

Car and Head of Vietravel delegation pick up at 190 Pasteur, District 3, Ho Chi Minh City, depart for the airport to pick up the trip to the city of London - also known as fog country (transit trip depending row).

Day 2:Edinburgh

Arrive Edinburgh airport, take the bus and take the delegation to visit the city: take a photo outside Edinburgh Castle - where was the main royal fort of Scotland, located on Castle Rock, a large volcanic rock area rose. an impressive way between the city of Edinburgh. Take a photo shoot in front of St Giles' Chapel, Princes Street Gardens - the road through the historic center of Edinburgh is now the city's main shopping city. After lunch at the local restaurant, continue to visit Holyrood Palace - magnificent palace built in the 12th century, views Calton Hill and New Town. Dine at the local restaurant and have a rest in the hotel.

Day 3:Edinburgh

Breakfast and check out hotel. You depart to visit the famous Glenkinchie Whiskey Factory of Scotland, free shopping local specialties. After lunch, depart for Manchester - the second largest city in England and is known as the "capital of the North of England". Have dinner and get hotel room to rest.

Day 4:Manchester - Cardiff

Breakfast and check out hotel. You take pictures at the Manchester Cathedral was built in the ninth century. Visit the famous Manchester United Football Stadium and Museum. Lunch at local restaurant. In the afternoon, you continue to move to the city of Cardiff - the capital of Wales. Have dinner and get hotel room to rest.

Day 5:Cardiff - Bath - Bristol

Breakfast and check out hotel. You visit Cardiff Castle was built in the middle of the century with typical Gothic architecture, stopping shooting in front of the Town Hall. Car to take you to the romantic city of Bath in the southwest of England. Group lunch at local restaurant. In the afternoon, visit the city of Bath: visit the Roman Baths, where thousands of years ago used to bath and spa for the Romans; The Royal Crescent, a semi-circular semi-colonial Georgian building, takes the Pulteney Bridge across the Avon River. Have dinner at local restaurant. Arrive in Bristol and check in hotel.

Day 6:Bristol - Stonehenge - London

Breakfast and check out hotel. Continue to Stonehenge. Visit Stonehenge - UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1986. Have lunch at local restaurant. In the afternoon, bring your car to the capital of London, free to visit and purchase souvenirs. Have dinner and get hotel room to rest.

Day 7:London - Windsor - London

After breakfast, take you to visit Windsor Castle - the largest castle in the world located in a beautiful land, the confluence of the River Thames and Kennet River located west of London. Have lunch at local restaurant. In the afternoon, the delegation returned to London. You visit the city: visit the Tower of London - historic monuments, masterpieces of world-famous architecture, is also a place to display Royal treasures. Buckingham Palace, London Tower Bridge, Big Ben Clock Tower, Parliament House, Wesminster Monastery - the site of the coronation of kings and queens, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Group dinner and return to the hotel to rest.

Day 8:London - Ho Chi Minh

Breakfast and check out hotel. Continue to visit London: British Museum - Museum of culture and human history with over 7 million historical artifacts. You are free to shopping until the time of appointment to the airport, take the flight to Ho Chi Minh City (Flight transit depending on the airline).

Day 9:Ho Chi Minh

Delegation to Tan Son Nhat airport, procedures entry Vietnam. The tour guide leaves the tour.
