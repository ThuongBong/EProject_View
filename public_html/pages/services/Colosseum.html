Details Itinerary

Day 1: Hồ Chí Minh - Rome

Car and guide Vietravel welcome you at Vietravel company (190 Pasteur, District 3, Ho Chi Minh city), departure to Tan Son Nhat airport for flight to Rome - capital of Italy (Flight too custom scene).

Day 2:Rome

Arrive Rome, the car and local HDV welcome visitors to take a photo outside the Coliseum, Trevi Fountain and Piazza Di Spagna Square, ancient Roman city, Patheon. Have lunch and dinner at local restaurant, get hotel room to rest.

Day 3:Rome

Have breakfast at Hotel. Morning visit to Vatican City: St. Peter's Square, St. Peter's Basilica, Vatican Museum. After lunch, the delegation shopping souvenirs at the local goods such as leather goods, glassware ... Dinner, the delegation to the hotel to rest.

Day 4:Rome - Pisa - Florence

After breakfast and check out hotel, depart for Pisa to visit the leaning tower of Pisa - recognized by UNESCO as World Heritage. Then, continue to visit the city of Florence: Take a photo outside Duomo II, visit the famous Ponte Vecchio bridge, Signoria square. Have dinner and get hotel room to rest.

Day 5:Florence - Venice

Breakfast and check out hotel. The tour of Venice is called the floating city. Here, you will visit the square and the church of Saint Marco, Murano glass factory, take a picture at the Doge Palace, take a canoe boat - Venice's typical transportation means (pay for yourselves , about 100Eu for one boat / 4 people). After dinner, the hotel team to rest.

Ngày 6:Venice - Milan - Hồ Chi Minh

After breakfast, depart for Milan, take a photo of the Castle of Sforza, Duomo cathedral, Galleria Vittorio Emanuele ... In the afternoon, depart for the airport to return to Ho Chi Minh City. (Non-scheduled Transit Flight)

Day 7:Hồ Chi Minh

Delegation to Tan Son Nhat airport, procedures entry Vietnam. The tour guide leaves the tour.
