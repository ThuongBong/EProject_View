Details Itinerary

Day 1: Hồ Chí Minh - Istanbul

You are at Tan Son Nhat airport according to instructions of the tour guide, the process of picking up flights to Istanbul-Turkey. Technical stop flights in Bangkok. You stay overnight on the plane.

Day 2:Ancient City Istanbul - Canakkale

Delegation to Istanbul International Airport, car and tour guides welcome. Lunch at restaurant, Depart to visit the city of Istanbul: Blue Mosque mosque - one of the most beautiful mosque in the world, visit the magnificent Hagia Sophia Cathedral now becomes a Museum. Then continue to visit the Topkapi Palace - a part of the historical population in Istanbul has been a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1985. The Topkapi Palace was built in the mid 15th century. This great palace can hold over 4,000 servants for the kings. The palace is home to the living, working, and entertaining of the Sultan emperors of the Ottoman Empire. Take a boat to cross the ocean currents Marmara to the Old City Troy. Have dinner at local restaurant. Overnight in Troy.

Day 3: Canakkale - Troy - Bergama - Kusadasi

Have breakfast at Hotel. Depart for visiting Troy Ancient Town. You will admire one of the famous heritage of the world - The Trojan horse famous in the Iliad. You will take a photo here. Next, visit the city, pass the ancient Roman Pergamon, ruins Temple of Trajan, Temple Athena - is the god of handicrafts, wisdom and at the same time is the god of war righteous. Agora & Pergamon Palace. In addition, you visit the ancient hospital famous Asklepion. Along the way, you enjoy the beauty of the Aegean beach, with olive trees planted on the island between Greece and Turkey. After lunch, we continue our journey to Kusadasi. If time permits, visit the apple tea factory in Kusadasi. Have dinner at local restaurant. Overnight in Kusadasi.

Day 4:Kusadasi - Ephesus - Pamukkale

Breakfast at hotel, car and guide Depart Depart to the ancient city of Ephesus known as where Mary and St. John lived and died here. Visit the ancient city of Selcuk and Ephesus with its stone buildings such as the Library of Celsius, Hadrian's Temple, Marble Street and Agora. Lunch at the local restaurant, continue to visit the Temple of Artemis worship goddess hunting, daughter Zeus - one of the seven wonders of the ancient world. Depart for Pamukkale, also known as Cotton Castle, located in southwestern Turkey. Unesco recognized as a World Heritage Site - with the fascinating beauty of the molten mineral springs from thousands of years ago to create the green lake on white rock, are the wonders of nature. unmatched in the world. You visit the remains of the Holy City Hierapolis - World Cultural Heritage (Unesco recognized in 1988). Take a photo of Cotton Castle. Have dinner at local restaurant. Get hotel room to rest.

Day 5: Pamukkale - Konya - Cappadocia

Have breakfast at Hotel. Depart for Konya - the ancient capital of Turkey in the 12th and 13th centuries. Visit the traditional Caravanserai Hotel with a 700-year history, (a stop on the legendary Silk Road). You visit Mevlana Monastery with blue conical architecture dome is the symbol of the city of Konya. Mevlana Monastery, also known as the Tomb of the Greatest Sage of Turkey, is respected and worshiped by the people. Continue your journey to Cappadocia. Check in at the hotel. Dinner, free time to relax.

Ngày 6:Cappadocia

Have breakfast at Hotel. In the early morning, you can register to take part in Cappadocia hot air ballooning - known as one of the best hot air balloon destinations in the world (self-paid). Depart for Cappadocia and visit the underground city Kaymakli - one of the 40 largest and deepest cities in the Cappadocia region - where an underground fortification system has been found. The food store is hidden deep in the cave. Continue to visit shopping at Turkey's largest handmade textile factory. Visiting Pigeon House - where a lot of pigeons and locals have made their homes deep into the cliffs for shelter and breeding pigeons. Watch the churches and stone chapels at the Goreme Open Air Museum. Uchisar Village is built on a hill, with houses, restaurants, hotels, shops ... deep in the cliffs that make up a unique residential neighborhood in the world. Continue to visit the ancient village of Avanos which still preserves two traditional trades known as carpet and ceramic. Finally, you have the opportunity to view the exhibition of turquoise emerald by the designer Marco Polo. Have dinner at the local restaurant, watch the traditional belly dance show of Turks (Cost self). Overnight in Cappadocia.

Day 7: Cappadocia - Ankara

Have breakfast at Hotel. Then, check the hotel and departure to Ankara - the capital of Turkey (on the way, you look at the picturesque pictures of salt fields as endless stretches). On arrival, visit the capital of Ankara with its magnificent Ataturk mausoleum and museum, the last resting place of Mustafa Kemal Ataturk - who is considered the father of modern Turkish construction. nowadays. Ataturk's tomb architecture is completely united with any mausoleum built according to the characteristic architecture of the Anatolian civilization. Have lunch and dinner at the local restaurant. Get hotel room at Ankara.

Day 8: Ankara - Istanbul

After breakfast, continue exploring the largest city in Turkey - Istanbul. You visit the Dolmabahce Palace - This European-style building was built by King Abdul Mecid in 1854 and was the residence of many kings. Today it is a museum and is used for important meetings, conferences, visits to the underground underground system of the great underground city of Istanbul - Underground Warter Cistern - The whole ceiling of the water palace This is supported by 336 large stone columns, creating beautiful dome, which can only be seen in churches, monasteries in Europe. This is a very monumental architecture with the only use is to store clean water for people's activities. Hippodrome Constantinople, 450 meters long and 150 meters long, was built in the third century AD during the reign of King Septimius Severus, with a capacity of over 40,000. Have dinner at local restaurant. Check hotel. Freedom to explore Istanbul at night.

Day 9:Istanbul - Hồ Chí Minh

Delegation to Tan Son Nhat airport, procedures entry Vietnam. The tour guide leaves the tour.
