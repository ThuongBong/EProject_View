Details Itinerary

Ngày 1:Ho Chi Minh - Phuket

Arrive at Tan Son Nhat International Airport for a flight to Phuket Island in southern Thailand. Arriving in Phuket, the local car and guide will pick you up and take you to dinner and check in hotel 3 * rest. Freedom to explore Phuket at night

Ngày 2:Beautiful Phi Phi Islands

Have breakfast at Hotel. Car and guide local delegation to the dock, visit the Phi Phi Islands - this is considered the island is famous for its tourism activities and beaches are known as one of the beach clean. best of the world. The Phi Phi Islands comprise six islands in which the largest is Phi Phi Don and Phi Phi Leh. The Phi Phi Islands are located 54km southeast of Phuket. Take a scenic tour around Phi Phi Leh Island with Green Bay Maya, Loh Samah Bay, Phi Leh Bay, Viking Cave - Pirates Cave. Free swimming at Ton Sai Bay and Monkey Sea with impressive blue jade water (if weather permits). Lunch buffet. You are free to explore Phi Phi Don island with a range of bars, souvenir shops featured. The ship returns you to the harbor. Dinner, you return to the hotel 3 * rest.

Ngày 3:Phuket

After breakfast, visit the beautiful beach city of Phuket: enjoy the morning sun on the beautiful Promthep Peninsula, visit Wat Chalong Monastery - is an indispensable place. To Phuket, the temple is highly revered by the local Buddhist community. Considered the most beautiful on the island, there is the gilded statue of Luang Por Cham, a monk who also became a hero when he helped quell the Chinese military invasion in 1876, great king of Rama. Have lunch at local restaurant. Continue your journey, visit local specialty shops to buy souvenirs for friends and relatives. Visit the Royal Gems Center to admire the beauty of the gem masterpieces of the people of Thailand in general and Phuket in particular. Have dinner and enjoy the special art show of the transsexual performer Simon Show. After the show, you return to the hotel.

Ngày 4:Phuket - Ho Chi Minh

Have breakfast. You are free to explore Phuket until the car and guide will take you to the airport to pick up flight back to Ho Chi Minh. Arrive at Tan Son Nhat airport, tour guide farewell. End the tour.
